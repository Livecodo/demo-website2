Crafting Memories,
One Crumb at a Time

Founded in a small kitchen with nothing but a rolling pin and a dream, Sweet Crumbs Bakery has grown into a local sanctuary for bread lovers and sweet tooths alike.

We believe that the best things in life take time. That's why our sourdough ferments for 48 hours, and our butter is sourced from local pasture-raised cows. No shortcuts, no preservatives—just honest, artisan baking.
